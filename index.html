HOW TO BUY $LIONKING

$LIONKING can be purchased and sold through PancakeSwap

Create MetaMask wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive $LIONKING.

Send $BNB to MetaMask

Transfer $BNB (BINANCE SMART CHAIN) to your new MetaMask wallet from your existing wallet (e.g. Trust Wallet, or Binance).

Connect Wallet to PancakeSwap

The safest place to buy $LIONKING is on PancakeSwap. Visit PancakeSwap and click “connect wallet” .MetaMask  will pop up and approve the connection.

Swap $BNB For $LIONKING

Enter the amount of $BNB you would like to swap for $LIONKING. Your $LIONKING will be Transferred to your MetaMask wallet.
